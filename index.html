FLL

FIRST LEGO League (FLL) is a STEM competition run by a nonprofit called FIRST (For Inspiration And Recognition of Science and Technology). Every year in September a challenge is released and thousands of teams work for hundreds of hours each to build a robot for the challenges, and solve a real world problem related to that years theme.

WRL

World Robotics League (WRL) is a robotics competition where in the spring and summer a challenge is released at the start of every month and teams bring their robots to compete at the end of the month.
